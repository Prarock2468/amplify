Eligibility Criteria

To join the program, startups must meet the following requirements

Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

The project should have a functional MVP demonstrating its core value proposition.

Seed Round Funding

Startups must have raised a seed round, with a public valuation exceeding $25 million.

Token Launch Readiness

The project should be ready to launch its token within the next 3 months.
